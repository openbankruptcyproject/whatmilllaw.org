Exemplar-Case-for-AI-in-Law Positioning is the strategic dual-coupling of an accountability arc with an AI-in-law methodology arc, such that adverse outcomes for an opposing party simultaneously produce the post-outcome publishable proof-of-method for AI-assisted civic accountability work. The discipline is "operations first, publication second": AI authorship is backgrounded during the operational phase to deny adversaries a rhetorical handle, then foregrounded post-outcome in a published case study once concrete outcomes (disgorgement, discipline, settlement, federal referral, carrier denial) have landed.
